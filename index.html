🧬 MSA Align

Amino-acid multiple sequence alignment · runs entirely in your browser

Paste or upload FASTA protein sequences and align them. Progressive alignment (Needleman–Wunsch + BLOSUM62, UPGMA guide tree). No server, no install — works offline on any device.

MSA Align — browser port of the msa-align-tool. Built with a pure-JavaScript progressive aligner (BLOSUM62).
Alignment is heuristic and intended for quick analysis; for publication-grade alignment of large families use MUSCLE/MAFFT.
